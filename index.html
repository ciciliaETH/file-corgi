A New Era of Decentralized Exchange on Filecoin
CorgiSwap emerges as a revolutionary platform on the Filecoin network, aiming to enhance performance, reliability, and lower fees for users compared to existing DEXs like SushiSwap. It overcomes challenges faced by SushiSwap, offering a seamless, secure, and efficient trading environment.

Competitive Fee Structure

CorgiSwap introduces competitive fees ranging from 0.1% to 1%, significantly lower than its predecessors. Fees are allocated to benefit the $Corgi holders: • 70% Buy & Burn: Increases liquidity, drives up token price, and expands total value locked (TVL). • 30% Development: Reinvested into platform development, covering centralized exchange listings, marketing initiatives, and participation in events to enhance visibility and attract more users.

Innovative Features and Future Roadmap

CorgiSwap's initial Version 1 includes essential features such as token trading and liquidity provision for passive income. Version 2's ambitious roadmap includes: • Bridge and Cross-Chain Functionality: Seamless asset transfers across different blockchain networks. • Staking and Yield Farming: Additional reward avenues and liquidity contribution. • Expanded Network Support: Incorporating major networks like Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, Solana, and Filecoin for enhanced interoperability and reach.
